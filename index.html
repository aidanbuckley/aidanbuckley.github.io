About

Before college, I built and ran Kicks Aidan, a sneaker resale business that generated over $60,000 in revenue across 350+ transactions and 400+ wholesale accounts. That was my Plan A — until I recognized the market declining in late 2022 and exited deliberately, closing at peak seasonal revenue. That decision led me to Fordham and to venture capital.

At Fordham's Gabelli School of Business, I'm pursuing a Finance major with a concentration in Alternative Investments. I'm active in the Alternative Investments Club's VC Group, and I've spent the past year building a real investment framework — not just coursework, but deliverables produced for actual funds.

My long-term goal is venture capital, with a secondary ambition to start a company. Both paths require the same foundational understanding: what makes a startup actually work. I'm building that understanding from both the operator and investor side simultaneously.
